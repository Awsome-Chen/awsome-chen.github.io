反三角函数

$$ d\,\frac{\arctan\left(x\right)}{d\,x}=\frac{1}{1+x^{2}} $$ $$ d\,\frac{\arccos\left(x\right)}{d\,x}=\frac{-1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}} $$ $$ d\,\frac{\arcsin\left(x\right)}{d\,x}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}} $$ $$ d\,\frac{\operatorname{arccot}\left(x\right)}{d\,x}=\frac{-1}{1+x^{2}} $$

三角函数

$$ d\,\frac{\tan\left(x\right)}{d\,x}=\sec^{2}\left(x\right)=\frac{1}{\cos^{2}\left(x\right)} $$ $$ \frac{1}{\sin^{2}\left(x\right)}=1+\cot^{2}\left(x\right) $$ $$ d\,\frac{\cot\left(x\right)}{d\,x}=\frac{-1}{\sin^{2}\left(x\right)} $$ $$ \tan^{2}\left(x\right)=\sec^{2}\left(x\right)-1 $$ $$ \csc\left(x\right)=\frac{1}{\sin\left(x\right)} $$ $$ \sec\left(x\right)=\frac{1}{\cos\left(x\right)} $$ $$ d\,\frac{\sec\left(x\right)}{d\,x}=\sec\left(x\right)\,\tan\left(x\right) $$
